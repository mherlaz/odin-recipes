Garlic Chicken

Description

This is a healthy but tasty reciper for meal prep

  • 4 chicken titties
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oil
  • garlic(powder,clove, doesnt matter)
  • honey
  • soy sauce
  • apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. season chicken titties
  2. bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes
  3. in a saucepan add soy sauce vinegar and honey
  4. on medium low heat stir until thick
  5. remove chicken titties from oven add sauce and voila
