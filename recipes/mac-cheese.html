Mac and Cheese

Description

This some good-ass mac and cheese for yo ass to sit down and eat with whatever main you want. Namaste

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cheese
  • 1lb elbow pasta
  • whole milk
  • butter

Steps

  1. boil pasta to al dente set aside
  2. small saucepan medium heat add butter 1 cup whole milk
  3. slowly add shredded cheese into roux whisk
  4. add elbows and sauce into oven safe pan cover with cheese
  5. bake 400 degrees 20-30 minutes or until golden brown
  6. bon appetit
