Chiles Rellenos

Description

This things be bomb as fuck yall don't even know! They're Poblano Peppers stuffed with milk cheese and fried in egg batter served with a tomato sauce eaten with a tortilla. Deliciouser than yo mama's cooking.

Ingredients

  • 2 Poblano Peppers
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tomato
  • half a slice of bread
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbs salt
  • half a pound of whole milk cheese
  • half a piece chicken bullion

Steps

  1. Roast peppers about 400 daquiris farenheit for about 45 minutes remove from oven and peel
  2. remove seeds
  3. open peppers and stuff with whole milk cheese
  4. separate egg whites whisk for an hour then agg yolk anyway
  5. cover peppers in flour then soak in egg wash
  6. prepare pan with oil medium high heat add eggs fry til golden brown
  7. cut tomatos in smaller pieces put in blender with garlic clove and chicken bullion.
  8. add water blend til watery consistency
  9. pour sauce into pan with chiles medium low heat bring to simmer
  10. heat some tortillas grab a modelo y a comer
